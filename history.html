The history !!

We are a 1960's Rock and Pop band that were originally formed in 1965. Our band members are Micky Dolenz, Davy Jones, Michael Nesmith and Peter "Petey" Tork. We have over fifty years experience in the music industry that started with our own tv show in 1966. Our tv show ran until 1968. We also starred in a film called "Head" in 1968. Since then, We have toured both nationally and internationally. We have produced 12 studio albums and have sold 75 millions records worldwide. Our first single was released in 1966 and our first album "The Monkees" spend 13 weeks at No.1 on the Billboard charts. We were inducted into the American Pop Music Hall of Fame in 2014 and have often been labelled as the pioneers of music video format. We are the only band to have four No.1 albums in a one year span and gave the Jimi Hendrix Experience their first concert tour exposure as a supporting act in 1967.
